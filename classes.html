Private Lessons Offered

Private Instruction is available from Skyhigh founder, Drago, both in studio and virtually. In studio private lessons are available for all levels of students on aerial silks, rope, hoop, chains, straps, aerial pole, and hammock. For students who have rigging at their homes, virtual classes are available on hourly basis.

Private lessons are great for absolute beginners, intermediate students working on sequencing, advanced students working on choreography, and anyone who wants to go deeper in their practice. While group classes are only available for adults over 18 years, private lessons and semi privates are available for all ages of aerialists.

Group Classes at SkyHigh Odditorium

A unique fusion of balance, focus, language, physics, and kinetics, effects of learning students of aerial silks include strength, deepened mind-body connection, and enhanced creativity. Regular group technique classes fuse traditional skills with modern variations, and encourage authenticity in movement. Schedule is supplemented with a weekly mixed level aerial conditioning class, focusing on alignment, flexibility and strength necessary for aerial arts.

Students are guided and encouraged to develop their own home practices. It is recommended to take both aerial technique and conditioning classes in the first year of practice.

As of September 2023, our group class schedule will greatly expand due to the graduation of our teacher trainers from their training course. We will be excited to offer the classes for kids, teens, and adults, as well as classes on aerial silk, aerial sling, aerial hoop (Lyra), conditioning & flexibility.
